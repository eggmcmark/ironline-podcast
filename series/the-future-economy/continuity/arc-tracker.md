## World State

- **Year**: 2126
- **Setting**: Active — eastern depopulated zone. Kirin and Dez on foot at Harrow Creek junction, waiting for contact from east. Next: Ep 5 ("The Reading Room").
- **Key world details**:
  - **The empty zone is curated.** Three factions: Outside maintenance (since 2112), Compact shadow budget, Curator. Sector-based (8 sectors, ~40 miles each). Established Ep 4.
  - **Salt is currency in the corridor.**
  - **Way-stations are nodes.** Salt, Larch depot, Harrow Creek post. Shared etiquette, not shared organization.
  - **The maintenance grid has a legible negative space.** Key is silent outside sensor coverage. Kirin is learning the grid's shape by where handshakes go absent.
  - **Authentication keys are smartcards.** Live, autonomous hardware tokens. The Adaeze line is a family of bearer chits.
  - **The Curator controls access.** Active intercepts. Operatives in the field. Reached dead-frequency comms at Salt.
  - **Compact infrastructure is porous.** Curator tap inside Compact-issued infrastructure housing. Boundaries are more porous than the Compact's framing admits.
