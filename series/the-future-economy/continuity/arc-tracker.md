- **Age**: Late 20s
- **Class**: Keeper
- **Occupation**: Materials analyst, Level Two, Sable River CapEx Board
- **State**: Grieving, resolute. Has decided to go east.
- **Key traits**: Reads surfaces (literally and metaphorically). Practical. Wry. Restless beneath competence.

- **Appeared on Ledger**: 2091
- **Died**: 3 weeks before story opens, age 89
- **True identity**: Unknown. Pre-Compact origins. Connected to Rootstock Protocol. Photo shows her young, radiant, in front of an unknown building. Holding an unknown child. "Coming home. 2089."

- **Role**: Senior materials analyst, 41 years at the Board
- **Key detail**: Saw one of these keys before, decades ago. The person who brought it never came back.

### Perun
- **Role**: Archivist at the Territorial Archive
- **Key detail**: Identified the Rootstock Protocol. Knows more than he's saying. Recognized the implications of the key immediately.

- **Role**: Kirin's best friend. Guild carpenter. Loyal, grounded, worried about her.
- **Key detail**: Knows about the autonomous maintenance systems in the empty zones. Told her to take a water filter.
