## Characters Introduced

### Kirin Saleh (protagonist)
- **Age**: Late 20s
- **Class**: Keeper
- **Occupation**: Materials analyst, Level Two, Sable River CapEx Board (on leave)
- **State**: End of Ep 3 — has crossed the frontier; in a vehicle with Dez heading east on an unmapped maintenance road; carrying both her own authentication key and Renata's chit; understands the key is a hardware authentication token doing handshakes with the curated infrastructure; knows Adaeze was active in September 2125 and died shortly after; knows there is an organized hunt on her line.
- **Key traits**: Reads surfaces (literally and metaphorically). Practical. Wry. Newly competent at lying. Beginning to think operationally.

### Adaeze Saleh née Okonkwo (deceased)
- **Appeared on Ledger**: 2091
- **Died**: 3 weeks before story opens, age 89
- **Pre-Compact identity**: Adaeze Okonkwo. Igbo surname. Member of a Rootstock plateau settlement (founder Nnedi). Witness on at least three Rootstock claim registrations 2078–2086.
- **Late life**: Active operator in the Sable River–east corridor as recently as September 2125. Came into the elder-care facility singing in Tamsat to dying residents AND went east to confirm enemy positions for an operation she may or may not have lived to see executed. Possible cause of death: walking too far at 89 in September. Possible cause of death: the Curator. Naila will not say.

### Hasse Lindgren
- **Role**: Senior materials analyst, 41 years at the Board.
- **Key detail**: Saw one of these keys before, decades ago. The person who brought it never came back. (Open thread — possible antagonist origin.)

### Perun
- **Role**: Archivist at the Territorial Archive. Formerly a Rootstock records specialist, western depopulated zone. Came into the Compact in 2093.
- **Key detail**: Witness on Adaeze's first Rootstock claim registration in 2078. Reads Tamsat (rustily). Was taught it by a woman named Ife. Currently translating the full letters off-screen.

### Tomas
- **Role**: Kirin's best friend since they were 14. Guild carpenter at a collective in a converted tram barn on the south edge of the Guild district.
- **Key detail**: Reads pre-Compact infrastructure records. Sources data cores from Perun. Outfitted Kirin for the journey. Told her to buy preserving salt before leaving — reason emerged on the road.

### Brigid Aelfgar

## World State

- **Year**: 2126
- **Setting**: Active — eastern depopulated zone. Kirin and Dez heading east on an unmapped maintenance road from Salt toward Larch depot.
- **Key world details established (Ep 3 rewrite)**:
  - **The empty zone is curated, not autonomous.** Has been for at least 14 years. Multiple factions direct the maintenance grid. The Compact's pastoral framing of "post-populated" is wrong, or at minimum incomplete.
  - **Salt is currency in the corridor.** Cannot be faked. Used for question fees, room and board, debts.
  - **Way-stations function as nodes.** Naila's Salt is one. The depot at Larch with Yusra is another. There is a shared etiquette but not a shared organization.
  - **Authentication keys are smartcards.** Hardware tokens with handshake protocols, not magical objects. The "Adaeze line" is a family of bearer chits; Kirin's is one, Renata's was another, there are more.
  - **The Curator is a real player.** Active intercepts on the corridor. Operatives in the field. Reach extends to dead-frequency comms.
  - **Compact infrastructure is partially Compact-issued.** The vehicle Naila gave Kirin and Dez has Compact tech in it. Boundaries between Compact and Outside are more porous than the Compact's framing admits.
