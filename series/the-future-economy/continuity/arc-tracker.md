## Characters Introduced

### Kirin Saleh (protagonist)
- **Age**: Late 20s
- **Class**: Keeper
- **Occupation**: Materials analyst, Level Two, Sable River CapEx Board (on leave)
- **State**: End of Ep 3 — has crossed the frontier; partnered with Dez; heading east; has been alone in the empty zone for the first time; wrote "everything here is running correctly. I am the only anomaly."
- **Key traits**: Reads surfaces (literally and metaphorically). Practical. Wry. Restless beneath competence.

### Adaeze Saleh née Okonkwo (deceased)
- **Appeared on Ledger**: 2091
- **Died**: 3 weeks before story opens, age 89
- **Pre-Compact identity**: Adaeze Okonkwo. Igbo surname. Member of a Rootstock plateau settlement. Witness on at least three Rootstock claim registrations 2078–2086. Came into the Compact to Sable River in 2089–2091, married into the Saleh name, worked twenty years as a Commons health aide at the elder-care facility, kept a small quiet life that was — in retrospect — a watch. Sang to dying residents in Tamsat.

### Hasse Lindgren
- **Role**: Senior materials analyst, 41 years at the Board
- **Key detail**: Saw one of these keys before, decades ago. The person who brought it never came back.

### Perun
- **Role**: Archivist at the Territorial Archive. Formerly a Rootstock records specialist in the western depopulated zone. Came into the Compact in 2093.
- **Key detail**: Witness on Adaeze's first Rootstock claim registration in 2078. Reads Tamsat (rustily). Was taught it by a woman named Ife. Lived in the same city as Adaeze for 33 years without knowing she was there. Currently translating the full letters off-screen.

### Tomas
- **Role**: Kirin's best friend since they were 14. Guild carpenter at a collective in a converted tram barn on the south edge of the Guild district.
- **Key detail**: Reads pre-Compact infrastructure records. Sources data cores from Perun. Gave Kirin a Guild canvas pack, a data core of infrastructure maps, and a warning about reservoirs. Worrying like wood drying in a kiln.

### Brigid Aelfgar
- **Role**: Kirin's supervisor at the CapEx Board. Founder-class assessor. 23 years in role.
- **Key detail**: Recognized Kirin was leaving before Kirin said it. Released her with grace. Classified her station as *Held at supervisor discretion* — a note she's used three times before; all three returned.

### Reva
- **Role**: Very old woman, late 90s, at The Bench (old-timers' club on Quarry Street). Walnut skin, white cloud of hair, sharpest eyes Kirin has ever seen on a frail person. Slight unplaceable accent.
- **Key detail**: Was at the plateau settlement as a visitor in 2081 when she was 19. Adaeze was teaching her to keep a Rootstock ledger. Came into the Compact in 2094. Has been in Sable River 15 years. Saw Adaeze across a plaza eight years ago and did not approach — "you do not expose a choice." Named the settlement's founder: Nnedi.

### Olu
- **Role**: Another member of The Bench. Thin, small, moss-colored sweater. Plays chess.
- **Key detail**: Left Kirin and Reva alone with grace — the club apparently does that kind of thing often.

### Lian Mwangi, Benno, Isa, Rufus
- Lian: host, cooks with her whole body. Fermentation cooperative. Kirin's long-time friend.
- Benno: husband, plays with Isa on the rug, bad at rice.
- Isa: their 18-month-old son. Still mispronounces Kirin's name as *Kee-rin* with a gap.
- Rufus: Commons teacher, Kirin's secondary-school cohort. Dropped the "empty for a reason" line.

### Amira
- **Role**: Wind-turbine maintenance specialist, Guild house on the east side. Rufus's partner. Dry, practical, articulate.
- **Key detail**: Lived three years in the eastern reach of the Compact's cultivated zone. Knows scavenger communities firsthand. Has seen a Chinese-state substation that isn't supposed to exist. Gave Kirin a second point-to-point comm, solar cells, a water filter better than Guild stock. Her comm is now the Sable River end of a backchannel between Kirin and home.

### Mira
- **Role**: Ghost-class night-shift aide at the Sable River Commons elder-care facility (west civic district). Fifties.
- **Key detail**: Worked alongside Adaeze. Told Kirin about Efe and the song. Efe stopped eating the day after Adaeze died.

### Dez *(new in Ep 3)*
- **Role**: Former Free Zone logistics coordinator. Has lived in the empty zone for eight years. Kirin's guide east.
- **Age**: Fifties
- **Background**: Seven years as logistics coordinator for a mid-sized Free Zone freight operation. Went automated; she became a human anomaly-override, pressing one button a month. Came out to the empty zone to think and stayed.
- **Speech patterns**: Direct. No wasted words. Technically precise about logistics, routes, distances. Dry humor — not jokes, but the shape of them. The reversal question ("what keeps you in Sable River?") is her default opening move with new arrivals.
- **Key detail**: She is positioned "at the edge of two maps" and has been watching the territory both maps fail to describe for eight years. Nobody goes east. That's why she's going. Has now received Kirin's second point-to-point comm (Amira's) and a copy of the infrastructure data core.

### Seun *(new in Ep 3)*
- **Role**: Former Compact structural engineer. 30 years in Guild certification. In the settlement eight years, arrived same week as Dez.
- **Key detail**: Thinks in load-bearing terms. Told Kirin: "most people going east aren't running from something, they're going toward something." Maintains the buildings.

### Tov *(new in Ep 3)*
- **Role**: Former Ghost-class musician from a large Free Zone city. In the settlement three years. Quiet, early twenties.
- **Key detail**: Knew Cleo before coming to the settlement. Her passphrase to Cleo is "the small musician sends her regards." Plays a small stringed instrument, music developed outside the Ledger's categories.

### Caramel *(new in Ep 3)*
- **Role**: Self-taught electronics specialist. Late forties. Self-given nickname.
- **Key detail**: Learned electronics from disassembled maintenance drones and salvaged pre-Compact manuals. Built a signal amplifier for Kirin's point-to-point comm (+30% range). Curious whether it functions 200+ miles out.

### Gren and Silber *(new in Ep 3)*
- **Role**: Supply-runners at the settlement.
- **Key detail**: Gren topped up Kirin's food supply. Silber repaired a separation beginning in Kirin's left boot heel.

### Cleo *(named only, not yet appearing)*
- **Role**: Woman living at the old rest stop on the northbound section, 60 miles east of the settlement.
- **Key detail**: Knows the country further east than anyone in Dez's settlement. Has history with Tov. Contact passphrase: "the small musician sends her regards."

- **Year**: 2126
