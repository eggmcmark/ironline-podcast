## Characters Introduced

### Kirin Saleh (protagonist)
- **Age**: Late 20s
- **Class**: Keeper
- **Occupation**: Materials analyst, Level Two, Sable River CapEx Board (on four weeks' leave)
- **State**: Moving. Has left Sable River before dawn on Saturday. En route east on the main highway.
- **Carrying**: Authentication key (inner jacket pocket), letters (cloth pouch), photograph, portable assay kit, Tomas's route summary, water filter, thermal layer, solar cell, 3 days rations, cherry wood toggle from Tomas
- **Key traits**: Reads surfaces (literally and metaphorically). Practical. Wry. Restless beneath competence. Increasingly aware of the gap between what the Compact can see and what exists.

### Adaeze (deceased)
- **Appeared on Ledger**: 2091
- **Died**: About 4 weeks before story opens, age 89
- **True identity**: Unknown full name (pre-Compact, begins with A). Was a coordinator of the builder community at the coordinates as of 2061. Significant figure. Left the community in 2089, one year before Compact formalized.
- **Known by**: Vashti (knew of her by reputation). Possibly known by Davorin if he's still alive.

### Hasse Lindgren
- **Role**: Senior materials analyst, 41 years at the Board
- **Key detail**: Knew Davorin — the Foundation Assessor who went east with a Rootstock key 40 years ago. Has been carrying this information for four decades.

### Perun
- **Role**: Archivist at the Territorial Archive
- **Key detail**: Found the Rootstock Registry. Gave Kirin a printed copy of the relevant fragment. Has been tracking this for decades. Nobody else ever asked.

### Tomas
- **Role**: Kirin's best friend. Guild carpenter. North district workshop.
- **State**: Worried but not obstructive. Compiled route documentation, sourced gear, made Kirin a cherry wood toggle for her pack strap.
- **Key details**: Knows the infrastructure records for the depopulated zone well. Expects status reports every 100 miles.

### Brigid
- **Role**: Foundation Assessor, Kirin's supervisor at the CapEx Board. Founder class.
- **Key detail**: Knows about the historical gap in the Compact's Sorting period records. Told Kirin to "come back with something real." She is the Compact's verification mechanism, waiting to receive what Kirin finds.

### Vashti
- **Role**: Old-timer, late 80s, lives in the south Commons center area. Ex-Free Zone — came to the Compact during formalization at age 22.
- **Key detail**: Knew of Adaeze by reputation. Knows the communities were called "the builders." Knows the letters are in a constructed community language. Did not give Adaeze's pre-Compact name — "that's hers to give, not mine." Wants to know what Kirin finds when she returns.
- **Speech patterns**: Direct, unhurried, unperformed. Uses simple declarative sentences. Doesn't apologize for what she says or doesn't say.

### Davorin (referenced, not present)
- **Role**: Former Foundation Assessor, Compact-born. Went east approximately 40 years ago with a Rootstock authentication key he found during a property survey.
- **State**: If alive, approximately 70-80 years old. Chose to stay at the community — resigned his Board position, gave up Founder status and Guild certifications. Sent one message to his sister six months after departure: "found something that made those things feel like instruments he no longer needed."
- **Significance**: If he's at the community, he's a bridge — a Compact professional who evaluated the builder world and chose it. Don't introduce too early.

### Kirin's social circle (minor, Sable River)
- **Roshan**: Structural engineer, Founder class, north district. Plans things eight years out. Comfortable in his life.
- **Hira**: Agricultural coordination, Roshan's partner. Thoughtful cook.
- **Jessa**: Commons secondary teacher. Warm, energetic. Cares about curriculum.
- **Preet**: Civil engineer, Guild-certified.
- **Calla**: Civil engineer, Guild-certified. Preet's partner.
