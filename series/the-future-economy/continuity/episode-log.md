| 2 | What She Left Behind | 2026-04-09 | 8,493 | ~47 min | Kirin Saleh (primary); brief close-third on Perun and Brigid | One long day in Sable River. Perun reveals he was a Rootstock witness himself in 2078 and recovers Adaeze's pre-Compact name: Adaeze Okonkwo. The turn comes when Mira, a Ghost-class elder-care aide, tells Kirin about a song Adaeze used to sing in a language none of them knew — *the road is long, but there is water at the end of it.* Kirin understands that her grandmother was not hiding but keeping a watch. She packs, stops at Adaeze's empty apartment at dawn, and leaves. |
