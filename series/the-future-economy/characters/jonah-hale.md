# Jonah Hale — The Inheritor

## Vitals
- **Born**: 2088
- **Season 3 age**: 10–30
- **Season 4 age**: 32–37
- **Occupation**: Foundation Assessor (the most prestigious technical role in the Compact)
- **Family**: Elena's son. Marcus's grandson. Mara's older brother.

## Physical
- Marcus's build, two generations refined. Broad shoulders he doesn't use for heavy labor anymore.
- Careful hands — an assessor's hands, used for measuring, testing, verifying. Not scarred like Marcus's.
- Wears the Foundation uniform on duty: plain, well-made, deliberately un-flashy. The Compact's aesthetic is competence, not display.

## Voice (speech patterns for dialogue)
- **Careful**. Measures his words the way he measures structures. Doesn't speak until he's sure.
- **Precise like Nadia, patient like Marcus**. The hybrid. Uses technical language naturally but can translate for anyone.
- **Questions are genuine**. Unlike Elena (weapons) or Nadia (probes), Jonah asks because he wants to understand.
- **Humor is self-deprecating**. Knows he was born into privilege. Uncomfortable with the Hale name's weight.
- **When angry, gets quieter**. The most dangerous version of Jonah is the one who stops talking.

## Psychology
- **Core trait**: He verifies. His entire professional life is about confirming that what claims to be real, is real. This becomes personal when the thing that's not real is his family's legacy.
- **Relationship to the Compact**: Believes in it the way a fish believes in water — without examining it. Until the anomalies.
- **Flaw**: Complacency disguised as competence. He has never had to fight for anything. The system worked for him. He hasn't tested his own convictions.
- **Blind spot**: He loves Mara. This makes him slow to see what she's doing. The Detroit anomaly he accepts too easily — that's the moment he'll regret forever.

## Key relationships
- **Mara**: His sister, his opposite, his mirror. He loves her and suspects she's destroying everything their grandfather built. The confrontation in Season 4 is the central scene of the entire series.
- **Elena**: His mother. She gives him his discipline and his mission. Her deathbed words haunt him.
- **Marcus**: He never met his grandfather. Marcus is a legend, a foundation protocol epigraph, a kitchen that still stands. Jonah measures himself against a man he only knows through the Ledger.

## Arc
Complacent inheritor → man who discovers the rot → someone who must choose between his family and the truth
