# Elena Hale — The Fighter

## Vitals
- **Born**: 2014, Brooklyn, NY
- **Died**: 2114 (age 100), on her deathbed, giving Jonah his mission
- **Season 1 age**: 13–26 (appears as child/teenager; becomes POV in Season 2)
- **Season 2 age**: 27–58
- **Occupation**: Civil engineer → policy attorney → Governor of the Northeast Compact Territory
- **Family**: Marcus's daughter. Mother deceased (before story begins). Two children: Jonah (b. 2088), Mara (b. 2090)

## Physical (as young woman, Season 2)
- Her father's hands — strong, capable, always doing something
- Shorter than Nadia, more compact. Built for endurance, not speed
- Dark hair, sharp jaw, her mother's eyes (Marcus sees this and it costs him)
- Dresses for the room she's in: work boots on job sites, tailored suits in the capital, her father's old flannel at home

## Voice (speech patterns for dialogue)
- **Sharp and strategic**. Every sentence has a purpose. She does not think out loud like Nadia — she thinks before speaking and speaks to win.
- **Shorter sentences than her father**, but more of them. Rapid-fire when arguing. Quiet when planning.
- **Uses questions as weapons**. "And what happens when the tithe is due and the assessment is wrong? Who pays? Show me."
- **Her father's directness without his patience**. She will cut you off. She is aware this is a flaw and does not care enough to fix it.
- **Swears rarely but effectively**. When Elena Hale swears, you know something has gone wrong.

## Psychology
- **Core trait**: She finishes things. Where Marcus starts and Nadia theorizes, Elena completes. She sees what needs to be built and builds it, whatever the cost.
- **Relationship to her father's idea**: She believes in it the way a soldier believes in the cause — totally, practically, without Marcus's philosophical ambiguity. This is her strength and her limitation.
- **Flaw**: Her ferocity forecloses gentleness. She wins every fight and some of those fights didn't need to be fought. She raises Mara in the aftermath of her victories, and Mara grows up seeing only the cost.
- **Blind spot**: She cannot imagine that someone who loves the Compact could also be right to challenge it.

## Key relationships
- **Marcus**: She worships him and doesn't know it. His death in 2061 is the hinge — she stops fighting for the idea and starts fighting for his memory, which is more dangerous.
- **Jonah**: Her steady child. The one who inherited Marcus's temperament. She trusts him.
- **Mara**: Her brilliant, difficult daughter. Elena sees herself in Mara and is frightened by it.

## Arc
Idealistic young engineer → ruthless political operator → old woman reckoning with the costs of winning
