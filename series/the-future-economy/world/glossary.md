# The Future Economy — Glossary (v4)

## Economic System
- **The Compact**: Post-automation economic framework. Value tied to real assets and physical production. Established ~2090.
- **EQU (Equity Unit)**: Currency backed by real asset appreciation. Ledger-only. No external exchange rate. Cannot be speculated on.
- **Equity participation**: Standard arrangement — contractors receive a percentage of property appreciation alongside or instead of cash.
- **Appreciation tithe**: 4% levy on appreciation events. Funds all public infrastructure.
- **Free Zone**: Non-Compact jurisdiction where financial abstraction is legal. Our economy, continued.
- **The Outside**: Network of communities that developed independently in the depopulated zones and elsewhere. Not hidden — just not on anyone's map.

## Institutions
- **CapEx Board (Capability Exchange)**: Regional registry matching skills to projects.
- **Value Ledger**: Distributed record of every material transaction. The economy's source of truth.
- **ASC (Asset Stewardship Council)**: Governing body organized by asset class.
- **Guild**: Certification body for skilled trades. Apprenticeship from 14, certification at 21.
- **Foundation Assessor**: Verifies that what claims to be real, is real. The Compact's immune system.
- **Commons Council**: Healthcare, education, public infrastructure. Perpetually underfunded.
- **Trade Board**: Manages barter-based cross-border trade with Free Zones.

## Social Classes
- **Founders (Builder Class)**: Highest status. Guild-certified across three domains. Increasingly hereditary.
- **Keepers**: Middle class. Property owners with moderate skill portfolios. Most people.
- **Abstract Class ("Ghosts")**: People whose work the Ledger can't verify. Growing population. The Compact's central tension.

## Locations (Season 1)
- **Sable River**: Mid-sized inland Compact city. Kirin's home. Built for more people than live there now.
- **Meridian**: Coastal Free Zone city. Loud, bright, chaotic. Our economy evolved.
- **The empty zones**: Depopulated regions between population centers. Infrastructure for 10B, inhabited by hundreds of thousands.
- **The frontier**: Where the Compact's Ledger coverage ends and the empty begins.
- **The transit point**: Edge of the Outside network. Maritime facility, repurposed.

## Characters (Season 1)
- **Kirin Saleh**: Late 20s. Keeper-class materials analyst at Sable River CapEx Board. The protagonist. Competent, practical, slightly restless, wry.
- **Adaeze**: Kirin's grandmother. Deceased. Quiet Keeper-class life that was apparently a cover for something much larger. Originally from the Outside.
- **Hasse**: Senior colleague at the CapEx Board. Forty years in. Recognizes the object.
- **Perun**: Archivist at the Territorial Archive. Knows what the authentication key is.
- **Tomas**: Kirin's best friend. Guild carpenter. Thinks she's insane.
- **Dez**: Former Free Zone logistics coordinator. Lives in the empty zone. Practical, dry-humored. Kirin's guide through the frontier.
- **Fen**: Monitors a relay station in the deep empty. First contact with the Outside network. Quiet, intense.
- **Dara**: Free Zone capability broker in Meridian. Charming, sharp, morally flexible.
