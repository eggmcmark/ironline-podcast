# The Future Economy — Glossary

Terms are introduced gradually through the story. This is the canonical reference for consistency.

## Economic System
- **The Compact** (also: "the Trade", derisively "the Guild World"): The post-automation economic framework built on real assets, embodied skill, and relational trust
- **EQU (Equity Unit)**: Global reserve instrument backed by rolling index of real asset appreciation. Cannot be printed — only earned by creating verifiable real-world value
- **Equity participation**: Arrangement where a contractor receives a percentage of property appreciation instead of (or in addition to) cash payment
- **Appreciation event**: Any transaction that establishes a new market value for a registered asset (sale, refinance, assessment)
- **Appreciation tithe**: 4% levy on appreciation events; funds all public infrastructure

## Institutions
- **CapEx Board (Capability Exchange)**: Regional registry replacing stock markets. Individuals post skills, projects post needs, matched algorithmically
- **Value Ledger**: Distributed record of every material transaction. Nothing abstract trades on it. Descended from blockchain
- **ASC (Asset Stewardship Council)**: Governing body organized by asset class (Land, Built, Productive Capacity, Commons)
- **Guild**: Certification body for skilled trades. Three-domain certification earns Founder status. Apprenticeship begins at 14, certification at 21
- **Foundation Assessor**: Guarantor of Value Ledger integrity. Certifies that claims of real value are, in fact, real. Part engineer, part auditor, part philosopher

## Social Classes
- **Founders (Builder Class)**: Highest status. Guild-certified across three domains. Can negotiate equity on any project
- **Keepers**: Middle class. Registered property owners with moderate skill portfolios
- **Abstract Class ("Ghosts")**: People living in AI-managed administrative layer, doing work the Ledger can't verify
- **Neo-Abstractors**: Faction engineering synthetic EQU instruments that technically pass audits but are divorced from real output

## Financial Instruments (Season 3–4)
- **Skill Forward**: Mara's invention. Derivative allowing EQU to flow in advance of completed work, based on Guild scores and completion rates. Technically legal. Philosophically antithetical to the Compact
- **Synthetic EQU**: EQU generated by derivative instruments rather than physical production. Indistinguishable in transactions from real EQU

## Trades and Construction (Season 1)
- **Millwork**: Custom woodworking — cabinets, moldings, built-ins
- **GC**: General contractor
- **Dovetail**: A strong interlocking wood joint cut at angles. A measure of craft
- **Quarter-sawn**: Lumber cut at a specific angle to the growth rings; more stable, more beautiful, more expensive
- **Load path**: The route that structural weight travels through a building to the ground
- **Header**: A horizontal structural member above an opening (door, window)
- **Plumb**: Perfectly vertical
- **Level**: Perfectly horizontal
- **Spec sheet**: Material specification document listing properties, grades, and load ratings

- **Danny**: Marcus's foreman. Twenty years together
